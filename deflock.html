You're Being Tracked
by Floyd County

It's not a conspiracy — it's reality.

Meet Flock Safety, the villian of our story

Flock Safety is a security company headquartered in Atlanta, GA that makes license plate reader (LPR) cameras for local governments and homeowners associations. Many other companies make similar cameras, like Motorola. But what does Flock Safety do differently? Flock doesn't delete the camera data. The data gathered by the cameras is logged into a database managed by Floyd County and Flock Safety: an actual location tracking database. These cameras know where you are and can draw your daily routine out for law enforcement or other nefarious actors if the data comes into the wrong hands.
